Vehicle Counting From Video Stream

It is simply an automated process that monitors traffic in a specific area and detects vehicles for further action. The captured images can provide valuable clues to cops and other public essential tracking ser- vices, such as the vehicle’s licence plate number, time and motion of the vehicle, details associated with the driver, and so on, all of which can lead to evidences of a crime or any unforeseen or unfortunate incidents. Previ- ously, images were manually processed.
